Phase roadmap
Phase 1 — FoundationWeeks 1–4
Learn the big compound lifts with light weight. Focus entirely on form. Start at 50–60% of what you think you can lift. Goal: perfect reps, not heavy weight. Add 2.5 kg/week upper, 5 kg/week lower if form holds.
Phase 2 — Load buildingWeeks 5–12
Progressively overload every session. Add weight when you complete all sets with 1–2 reps still in reserve. Track every lift. This is where real beginner gains happen — treat every session as a PR opportunity.
Phase 3 — HypertrophyWeeks 13–24
Alternate heavy weeks (4–6 reps) with volume weeks (8–12 reps). Introduce deload every 6–8 weeks. By month 6, you'll be unrecognizable — stay the course.
