Phase roadmap
Phase 1 — FoundationWeeks 1–4
Learn the big lifts with light weight. Focus 100% on form — squat, deadlift, bench, OHP. Start at 50–60% of what you think you can do. Perfect reps only.
Phase 2 — Load buildingWeeks 5–12
Progressive overload every session. Add weight when all sets are complete with 1–2 reps left in the tank. Track every lift. This is where real beginner gains explode.
Phase 3 — HypertrophyWeeks 13–24
Alternate heavy weeks (4–6 reps) with volume weeks (8–12 reps). Deload every 6–8 weeks. By month 6, you will be unrecognizable.
